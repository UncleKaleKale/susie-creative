Subject Matter

The first half of this course is how to draw what we see. Using Betty Edwards' materials, students will learn to let go of the "Symbol System," the natural inclination to draw from memory, and instead draw from what their eyes behold.

In-Class Exercise:

  • Pre-Instruction Self-Portrait (20-30 minutes)
  • Value Scales, starting with #2 pencil…
  • Begin In-Class Exercise. Complete at Home. Watch Video 1.
